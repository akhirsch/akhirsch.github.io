Research Interests

My research focuses on the uses of semantics in computer science, especially the denotational semantics of programming languages, systems, and logics. I'm currently working with Professor Ross Tate on categorical semantics for type-and-effect systems, and with Professor Bob Constable on type-theoretic models for constructive logics.

Research Projects

  • Intuitionistic Model Theory
  • Semantics of Type-And-Effect Systems

Activities

I currently serve as the PLDG Czar, which is the large programming language reading group here at Cornell. In addition, I'm the founding TPLS Czar, which is the Theory B/Eurotheory/Semantics reading group here.
